#Ancients 

The High Folk that was the pseudo ring master of the tower. Changes voice every time he speaks; multiple voices speak at once when annoyed/commanding. Slightly eccentric.

Description:
A metallic dragon-- seemingly silver, but in a process of continual melting, its eyes pure black and white. Can extend appendages out that look like other species (a human arm from its back, for example)

Soul: An undulating mass, similar to a silk cocoon of sorts, with strings attaching its many hands and arms.
