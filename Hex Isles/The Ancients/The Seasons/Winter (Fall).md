Group/Faction/Controlling Entity of [[The Seasons]]. Resides in [[The Ancients|The Ancient's]] territory.

Language Rule: Dont talk to them directly, dont look at them, etc etc. Act as if they dont exist. However, if you do need to talk with them, as always, you can imply what you want to / mean to say.
Ex:
**Folk:** Stalking / Slowly following person.
Person: I have an offering of food. *While setting the food down in front of them.* I would want a wagon wheel for this offering, if at all possible. *They walk past the food. The folk takes it, and after the person knows the folk has left, they take what the folk gave them.*

#Ancients 

Payment: Biological Materials; Blood, Bones, Etc.
Punishments: Bodily Harm.
