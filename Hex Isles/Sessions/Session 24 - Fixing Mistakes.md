The landscape slowly transforms to a sprawling forest as the party continues forth, various trails leading further in, seemingly cut at random. The ground is brittle and slick with frost, though its only cold in very specific spots. After walking for 10 or so minutes, they arrive at a small clearing, with a cart in the center, a sign at the front drawn in ink, "Trading - Honor System." At the cart stands Espes, currently trading and putting items back on their own cart.

[[Tigoon]] will notice that more of the shards of his [[Soul]] are missing.

If the party *does not* make themselves known, Espes will finish trading within a few minutes before leaving on one of the many trails out.

If the party does, Espes will look shocked.

"Tigoon?!-- How did-- why are you guys here?"

Espes will be hesitant to say specifically why he's here, just saying that he's here to "fix" something. If pressured (or if Tigoon tricks him into admitting, or if the party refuse to leave), he'll crack and say that he's here to get [[Elizabeth]] back.

"If I acted differently, she might not of lost her memory. I messed up, and I'm fixing it. But you guys don't need to be here for that-- especially not *you*. You're done!-- you're done "adventuring", you visited the factions, you can be done and you can go home, and you can be *safe.*"

After a tense conversation, Espes finally agrees to let the party help, with some initial pushback. They get to the cart and Espes tells them the barebones idea they have. Have the High Folk return Elizabeth to the outside through trading. "They aren't amazing at determining something's worth. I think trading my violin will work. Its one of a kind-- It'd take me a few months to get something like it back. Which is around the same amount of time it takes to create a new kobold. I wouldn't be surprised if they presume they're of equal worth because of that."

After a day or so of travelling, the party finds themselves back at the endless dirt roads they entered the tower from previously. Entering the area (with the help of Espes), they're once again in the clearing below the tower. Its very empty this time; no [[Fae Folk|Folk]] in sight. Either [[Ferno (Folk)|Ferno]] will request audience with the high folk, [[Espes]] will attempt to get its attention through song, or [[Tigoon]] will fly everyone up to the top.

	"Almighty all, we request you heed our message and listen to our call."

[[Everything]] denies the bargaining, saying that the material items won't be able to run its game like [[Elizabeth|The Ringmaster]] can. 

	"Would you be willing to trade this one?" Points to Tigoon.


If [[Tigoon]] at any point attempts to enter his [[Narrative Space|headspace]], skip down. Otherwise, after a bit of tense discussion back and forth, Foogoo will bite on [[Tigoon]]'s leg, sending him into the space.

Cutting back to the rest of the party, you all see a black mass suddenly start at [[Everything]]'s chest, before spreading outwards at an alarming rate. Titan lets out a screech of pain, and drops to the floor. The tower begins to crack, and the sky flickers to a pitch black, before the mass reaches all you. Even trying to fly away, black tendrils launch out of the ground, grabbing onto whoever tried to escape.

Its silent, for a while. As the mass reached you all, every sense that you all possessed was quickly muted. Not necessarily "unconsciousness", but like you just... weren't there, for a while.

And then you all get your sight back. (For Dashel that means Titan).

You find yourselves in an area roughly the same as before. Everything is pitch black, apart from all of you, and vague, white outlines compose the objects and ground.
