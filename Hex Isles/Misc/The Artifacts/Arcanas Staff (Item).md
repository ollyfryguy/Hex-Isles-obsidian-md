Artifact currently controlled by [[Arcanas Staff]]. Controlled in a dungeon 100 meters down, in the 3 way border intersection point between [[Sector G, The Staff's Auditorium]], [[Sector D, Diviners]], and [[Sector E, Village of Mercenaries]]. Is stored in a pocket dimension, which can only be accessed through 6 of the 9 of the prior [[The Council|council]] members. (Or if 6 of the council members die to... unfortunate circumstances, less.)

#### Item Description & Effect
Is responsible for the containment and subduing of the various [[Great Fears|fears]] and their respective avatars.
