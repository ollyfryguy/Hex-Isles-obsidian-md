Also called Filth, The Crawling Rot, and Flesh Hive.

The Corruption is the fear of corruption, disease, and filth. Fear of the feelings of disgust, revulsion, and the things or beings that might evoke such feelings.

Manifests as mold, bugs, rot, decay, infection, the feeling of one's skin crawling; can also manifest as unhealthy love and companionship.
