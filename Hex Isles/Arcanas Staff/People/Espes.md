#Arcanas_Staff #Ancients 

A guide and traveler to [[The Ancients|The Ancient's]] territory.

Relevant Notes:
[[Session 9 - Consistent Inconsistency]]

Pronouns: He/They

Physical Description:
They're a relatively short catfolk, around 5'3, 5'4, with white and brown fur, along with a shortsword sheathed at their side and a backpack thats at least half the size of them. They have a nametag poorly sewn onto their vest, which reads in faded letters "Espes"

Notes:
Currently hired by [[Chariot Tyr]] and lives in [[Sector A, Drop Off Point]].

Had a past history with [[Elizabeth]]; escorted her around [[The Ancients]] when she first arrived and helped her survive.
