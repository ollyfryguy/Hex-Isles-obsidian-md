#Architects 

A 4'8 dwarf, currently residing in the [[2nd Layer]] of [[Torkin]]. 

Pronouns: He/Him

Physical Description: The right half of his face is replaced with a brass faceplate, his eye socket in tow replaced with a bright green LED. His right hand is replaced with a smithing hammer, made of adamantine. His left hand can fold and descend inwards, replaced with various tools.

Worshipper of Hephaestus, with his workshop serving as a dual worship grounds for him.
