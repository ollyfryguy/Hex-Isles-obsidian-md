The personal room / workshop of the [[Rhali Sine]]. The room is mostly shades of dark purples, blacks and velvet reds. Clothes of various kinds line the walls: dresses, mantles, shirts; if you can name a clothing item chances are its probably in here. On the right wall you see a standing mirror, a wardrobe with supposedly even more clothes, and a tailoring desk. On the left, an elegant bed and bedframe, chest, and finally a chair and a small circular table. On said table is a paper note, and in, rather messy cursive, the words "Be back soon!~"
